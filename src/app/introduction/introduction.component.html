CodeStream Introduction

CodeStream has a combined 40 years experience in Information Technology designing and building systems for large enterprises and small startups.

We specialize in software development of line of business applications (web and mobile) using Microsoft technologies.

We are full stack developers, highly skilled in Microsoft technologies and the Azure cloud platform. We have extensive experience in object orientated architecture, systems design and development as well as coding practices, specifically test-driven development, agile and XP approaches.

We have experience in various industries and sectors as well as all aspects of the software development lifecycle from project conception through analysis, design and implementation and finally, to deployment, maintenance and support of the final product.
